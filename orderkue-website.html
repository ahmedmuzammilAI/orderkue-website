FAQ

Do customers need an app?

No. Customers stay on WhatsApp. KueBot chats with them there.

What do I need to start?

Just your WhatsApp number and your catalog/menu. We set KueBot up.

Do I still control the order?

Yes. KueBot collects details and suggests options. You confirm on the dashboard.

Can KueBot suggest products?

Yes. KueBot can recommend items from your catalog based on what the customer needs.
